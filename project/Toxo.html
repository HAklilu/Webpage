Biology Math Prototype

Currently we are at a summer program at Wharton. We are doing project based classes. Our project is to find a solution to a communicable disease. We chose toxoplasmosis, which is a parasitic disease that mainly affects people with weak immune systems. Our goal is to find a way to reduce the number of people infected with toxoplasmosis in the Philadelphia area. As a team, we are making sure that our research, data, and solution are trustworthy.

The Toxoplasma Gondii parasite more commonly known as Toxoplasmosis is a common and easily transmitted disease. The most common way it is transmitted is through cat feces but it can also be transmitted through contaminated foods or water. The parasite is asymptomatic but few might feel mild flu like symptoms such as muscle ache. Toxoplasmosis is most harmful in pregnancies and people with weak immune systems.
