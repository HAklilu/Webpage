    Biology Math Prototype

asymptomatic: does not show symptoms

parasite: Lives at the expense of other organs.

Microscopic Organisms: Tiny life forms that only have one cell.

Organelles: the structure within a cell

Oocyst: An organ (cyst) in an animals body that contains paracites

Definitive Host: An organism that supports the adult or sexually reproductive form of a parasite.

Intermediate Host: An organism that supports the immature or nonreproductive forms of a parasite.

Tachyzoites: A rapidly multiplying stage in the development of the tissue phase of certain coccidial infections, as in Toxoplasma gondii development in acute infections of toxoplasmosis.

communicable disease: a disease that can be transmitted

conoid: Having to do with zoology andd cone in shape
